# PIECES SYSTEM

---

# 1. OVERVIEW

Pieces combine:

- Chess movement
- Combat attributes

---

# 2. ATTRIBUTES

Each piece has:

- Attack Dice (AD)
- Defense Dice (DD)
- Health Points (HP)

---

# 3. STATS TABLE

| Piece  | AD | DD | HP |
| ------ | -- | -- | -- |
| Pawn   | 2  | 1  | 1  |
| Knight | 3  | 2  | 2  |
| Bishop | 3  | 2  | 2  |
| Rook   | 4  | 3  | 3  |
| Queen  | 4  | 3  | 4  |

---

# 4. DESIGN ROLES

---

## Pawn

- Disposable unit
- Disruption tool

---

## Knight/Bishop

- Tactical fighters

---

## Rook

- Durable frontline

---

## Queen

- High-impact flexible unit

---

# 5. BALANCE INTENT

- Strong pieces = consistency
- Weak pieces = volatility
- HP enables long-term trades
