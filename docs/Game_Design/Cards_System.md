# CARDS SYSTEM

---

# 1. OVERVIEW

Cards modify gameplay through:

- Buffs (self)
- Debuffs (enemy)

---

# 2. DISTRIBUTION

Each player receives:

- 1 Buff
- 1 Debuff (applied to opponent)

---

# 3. RULES

- Permanent
- Non-stackable
- Bounded effects

---

# 4. DESIGN PURPOSE

- Create asymmetry
- Influence decisions
- Modify combat indirectly

---

# 5. TYPES

---

## Buffs

- Enhance player performance

---

## Debuffs

- Introduce risk to opponent

---

# 6. LIMITS

- Max +2 damage
- Max 20% probability
- No infinite scaling
