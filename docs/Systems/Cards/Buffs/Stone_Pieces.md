# Stone Pieces

---

# 1. Card Type

Buff

---

# 2. Category

Aggressive / Consistency

---

# 3. Effect

Whenever an allied piece deals damage in normal combat, its total damage cannot be lower than:

```txt
2 damage
```

---

# 4. Trigger

Triggers after normal damage calculation if:

- The allied piece dealt at least 1 damage.
- The final damage total is lower than 2.

---

# 5. Target

The enemy piece damaged by the allied piece.

---

# 6. Duration

Entire match.

---

# 7. Restrictions

- Does not trigger if the attack deals 0 damage before Minimum Damage Rule.
- Does not affect Divine Attack.
- Does not affect the King.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
- Does not stack with other minimum damage effects.
- Does not increase damage beyond the minimum threshold unless another effect applies.

---

# 8. Design Intent

Stone Pieces improves damage consistency.

It is designed for players who want reliable combat outcomes instead of explosive but uncertain damage.

---

# 9. Strategic Use

This Buff encourages:

- Frequent attacks
- Reliable trades
- Pressure through consistent damage
- Aggressive mid-game combat

---

# 10. Counterplay

The opponent can respond by:

- Avoiding unnecessary trades
- Using high-HP pieces to absorb damage
- Forcing the Stone Pieces player into unfavorable positions
- Reaching Frenzy, where the Buff no longer matters

---

# 11. Example

```txt
White Bishop attacks Black Knight.

Combat damage = 1

Stone Pieces increases minimum damage to 2.

Black Knight takes 2 damage.
```

---

# 12. Balance Notes

Risk:

- May make low-damage attacks too efficient.

Controls:

- Requires damage to occur first.
- Does not apply to failed attacks.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
- Does not affect the King.
