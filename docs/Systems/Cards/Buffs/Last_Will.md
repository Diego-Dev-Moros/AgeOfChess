# Last Will

---

# 1. Card Type

Buff

---

# 2. Category

Retaliation / Defensive

---

# 3. Effect

When an allied piece dies in normal combat:

```txt
Deal 2 damage to the enemy piece involved in that combat.
```

---

# 4. Trigger

Triggers after combat damage is applied and an allied piece dies.

---

# 5. Target

The enemy piece involved in the combat that caused the death.

---

# 6. Duration

Entire match.

---

# 7. Restrictions

- Only triggers from normal combat death.
- Does not trigger from Frenzy captures.
- Does not affect the King.
- Does not trigger from terrain-only damage unless explicitly allowed.
- Does not trigger if no enemy combat piece exists.
- Does not stack.

---

# 8. Design Intent

Last Will makes aggression against the player more dangerous.

It gives value to sacrifices and allows dying pieces to still influence the board.

---

# 9. Strategic Use

This Buff encourages:

- Sacrificial trades
- Defensive traps
- Holding contested squares
- Punishing enemy attacks

---

# 10. Counterplay

The opponent can respond by:

- Avoiding low-HP attackers
- Using ranged positional pressure
- Capturing during Frenzy
- Attacking with pieces that can survive retaliation
- Forcing Last Will pieces into bad positions

---

# 11. Example

```txt
White Knight attacks Black Bishop.

Black Bishop dies in combat.

Black has Last Will.

White Knight takes 2 damage after combat resolution.
```

---

# 12. Balance Notes

Risk:

- Can create many mutual deaths.

Controls:

- Damage is fixed at 2.
- Only triggers on combat death.
- Does not affect the King.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
