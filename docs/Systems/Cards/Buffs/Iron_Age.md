# Iron Age

---

# 1. Card Type

Buff

---

# 2. Category

Defensive

---

# 3. Effect

All allied pieces defend using their Attack Dice value instead of their Defense Dice value.

Example:

```txt
Rook normally:
AD = 4
DD = 3

With Iron Age:
Defense Dice = 4
```

---

# 4. Trigger

Triggers whenever an allied piece is defending in normal combat.

---

# 5. Target

All allied non-King pieces.

---

# 6. Duration

Entire match.

---

# 7. Restrictions

- Does not affect the King.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
- Top 3 Dice Rule still applies.
- Does not increase HP.
- Does not directly reduce incoming damage.
- Does not stack with other Defense Dice increases.

---

# 8. Design Intent

Iron Age supports a defensive playstyle.

It makes holding positions stronger and makes enemy attacks less predictable.

---

# 9. Strategic Use

This Buff encourages:

- Defensive formations
- Holding key squares
- Counterattacking after enemy failure
- Protecting high-value pieces

---

# 10. Counterplay

The opponent can respond by:

- Avoiding direct attacks
- Using Giant Slayer-like effects
- Forcing trades during Frenzy
- Attacking with stronger pieces
- Using positioning instead of direct combat

---

# 11. Example

```txt
Black Knight is attacked.

Knight normally:
AD = 3
DD = 2

With Iron Age:
Knight defends with 3 dice.

Black rolls 3 Defense Dice instead of 2.
```

---

# 12. Balance Notes

Risk:

- May slow down combat too much.

Controls:

- Top 3 Dice Rule limits large dice pools.
- Does not increase HP.
- Does not protect against checkmate.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
