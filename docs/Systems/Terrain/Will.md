# Will

---

# 1. Terrain Type

Scaling

---

# 2. Effect

At the start of the match, all non-King pieces gain:

```txt
+1 HP
+1 damage
```

This applies to both players equally.

---

# 3. Trigger

Triggers once at the start of the match.

---

# 4. Affected Pieces

All non-King pieces from both players.

---

# 5. Duration

The HP bonus lasts until the piece dies or Frenzy begins.

The damage bonus lasts until Frenzy begins.

---

# 6. Restrictions

- Does not affect the King.
- Applies only once.
- Does not stack.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
- Does not modify dice values.
- Damage bonus must respect global damage caps.

---

# 7. Design Intent

Will creates a stronger and more explosive battlefield.

It increases both survivability and damage output, making pieces more dangerous while also allowing them to survive longer.

The goal is to create a match with larger tactical swings and more meaningful combat decisions.

---

# 8. Strategic Impact

This Terrain encourages:

- High-impact combat
- More durable pieces
- Stronger trades
- Aggressive mid-game decisions
- Tactical calculation before engaging

Because pieces gain both HP and damage, fights can become more decisive but also riskier.

---

# 9. Counterplay

Players can adapt by:

- Avoiding careless trades
- Targeting weakened pieces
- Using high-value pieces carefully
- Creating check threats instead of relying only on damage
- Preserving material until Frenzy

---

# 10. Example

```txt
White Knight normally has:

HP = 2

With Will:
HP = 3

If White Knight deals damage:
It deals +1 additional damage.
```

---

# 11. Interaction with Buffs

Will can increase the impact of offensive Buffs.

Examples:

- Stone Pieces + Will improves damage consistency.
- Giant Slayer + Will can create powerful underdog attacks.
- Last Will may become more dangerous if retaliation damage is modified by global damage effects.

Damage caps must still be respected.

---

# 12. Interaction with Debuffs

Will can soften HP-reduction effects.

Example:

```txt
Black Rook:
Base HP = 3

Will:
HP = 4

Crystal Pieces:
HP -1

Final HP = 3
```

---

# 13. Interaction with Frenzy

During Frenzy:

- All HP becomes 1.
- Damage calculation is disabled.
- Will stops applying.

---

# 14. Balance Notes

Risk:

- Can make combat too explosive when combined with damage Buffs.

Controls:

- Applies only once.
- Does not affect the King.
- Disabled during Frenzy.
- Damage caps still apply.
