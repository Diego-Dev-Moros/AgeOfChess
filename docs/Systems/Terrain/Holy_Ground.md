# Holy Ground

---

# 1. Terrain Type

Recovery

---

# 2. Effect

For each team, the first 3 allied piece deaths trigger:

```txt
All remaining allied non-King pieces gain +1 HP.
```

Each team tracks its own Holy Ground triggers separately.

---

# 3. Trigger

Triggers when an allied non-King piece dies.

---

# 4. Affected Pieces

All remaining allied non-King pieces of the player whose piece died.

---

# 5. Duration

The HP gained is permanent until the piece dies or Frenzy begins.

The effect can trigger up to:

```txt
3 times per team
```

---

# 6. Restrictions

- Does not affect the King.
- Does not trigger during Frenzy.
- Does not trigger after the team has already used 3 Holy Ground triggers.
- Does not revive dead pieces.
- Does not affect pieces that died in the same combat before the trigger resolves.
- HP bonuses are removed when Frenzy begins because all HP becomes 1.

---

# 7. Design Intent

Holy Ground turns sacrifice into resilience.

It makes death meaningful beyond losing material and allows a player to absorb early losses by strengthening remaining pieces.

The goal is to create a battlefield where sacrifice can create long-term durability.

---

# 8. Strategic Impact

This Terrain encourages:

- Sacrificial trades
- Defensive setups after losing pieces
- Mid-game resilience
- Protecting fewer but stronger surviving pieces
- Aggressive exchanges if the player can benefit from death triggers

---

# 9. Counterplay

Players can adapt by:

- Avoiding low-value trades that empower the opponent
- Targeting already-buffed pieces carefully
- Forcing deaths close to Frenzy, where HP bonuses soon disappear
- Using high-damage attacks to kill important pieces despite HP boosts
- Prioritizing check threats over material trades

---

# 10. Example

```txt
White Bishop dies.

White has used 0 Holy Ground triggers so far.

Holy Ground triggers:
All remaining White non-King pieces gain +1 HP.

White Holy Ground triggers used:
1 / 3
```

---

# 11. Multiple Deaths in One Combat

If multiple allied pieces die as part of the same resolution window:

- Each death may trigger Holy Ground separately.
- The trigger limit of 3 per team still applies.
- Dead pieces do not receive the HP bonus.

Example:

```txt
Two White pieces die in the same resolution.

White has 1 trigger remaining.

Only 1 Holy Ground trigger occurs.
```

---

# 12. Interaction with Buffs

Holy Ground can combine with defensive Buffs.

Examples:

- Iron Age + Holy Ground creates durable defenders.
- Last Will + Holy Ground rewards sacrificial trades.
- Stone Pieces + Holy Ground creates stronger attrition loops.

All interactions must remain bounded by the 3-trigger limit.

---

# 13. Interaction with Debuffs

Crystal Pieces can reduce the impact of Holy Ground by lowering base HP.

Fatigue may accidentally trigger Holy Ground through friendly damage if it causes a piece to die.

---

# 14. Interaction with Frenzy

During Frenzy:

- All pieces HP become 1.
- Holy Ground stops triggering.
- Previous HP bonuses are ignored.

---

# 15. Balance Notes

Risk:

- Can create snowballing durability if unlimited.

Controls:

- Limited to first 3 deaths per team.
- Does not affect the King.
- Does not apply during Frenzy.
- Does not revive pieces.

---
