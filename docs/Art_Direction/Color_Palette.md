# Color Palette

---

# 1. Purpose

This palette defines the color language for Age of Chess assets.

The palette should be limited enough to keep pixel art cohesive, but broad enough to support cards, terrain, factions, dice, and UI states.

---

# 2. Base Palette

| Role | Colors | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Deep shadow | `#080A0B`, `#111817`, `#1B2421` | Card interiors, piece undersides, board cracks |
| Stone gray | `#3A3E42`, `#555B5D`, `#777E7A`, `#A8B0A7` | Neutral pieces, ruins, dice bodies |
| Parchment | `#E7C985`, `#F0DDA7`, `#FFF1C2` | Card nameplates and text boxes |
| Antique gold | `#8C6324`, `#C4923A`, `#F0C95A` | Frames, rare highlights, terrain reward accents |
| Arcane green | `#1F8A4C`, `#35C56F`, `#8CFFB1` | Buffs, healing, growth, active magic |
| Arcane blue | `#245D8F`, `#3CA7D9`, `#9DE7FF` | Protection, dice magic, holy-cold highlights |
| Blood red | `#6B1618`, `#B9342B`, `#FF6A4D` | Damage, hell terrain, danger |
| Corrupt purple | `#37184F`, `#7131A4`, `#C27CFF` | Debuffs, corruption, distortion |

---

# 3. Accent Colors

Use accents in small quantities:

- Bright white `#FFFBE2` for highest magical glints.
- Toxic green `#91E36A` for corrupted terrain only.
- Ember orange `#F47A32` for hell terrain only.
- Royal teal `#2DB6A3` for dice and arcane UI effects.

Accent colors must not become the dominant background color unless the asset category specifically requires it.

---

# 4. Card Type Colors

| Card Type | Border | Glow | Background |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Buff | Green or blue with gold trim | Green, blue, or white | Dark neutral with controlled particles |
| Debuff | Purple or red with tarnished trim | Purple, red, or sickly green | Dark, colder, more oppressive |
| Terrain Buff | Gold with environment color | Gold or blue | Environmental but low-noise |
| Terrain Debuff | Blackened metal, red, or purple | Red, purple, toxic green | Environmental hazard language |

---

# 6. Faction Colors

Light faction:

- Primary: pale stone and ivory.
- Secondary: antique gold.
- Accent: blue-white or warm white glow.

Dark faction:

- Primary: basalt, blackened metal, and dark stone.
- Secondary: tarnished gold or muted green.
- Accent: emerald, purple, or red-violet glow.

Faction colors must remain distinguishable for colorblind players through value and silhouette, not hue alone.
